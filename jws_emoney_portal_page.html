Built like a bank vault

Security is the whole point.

eMoney is used by tens of thousands of financial firms because it holds up to the same security standards as the institutions where your money already lives.

End-to-end encryption

Your data is encrypted both in transit and at rest, using the same standards used by major financial institutions.

Two-factor authentication

An extra verification step every time you log in from a new device, so a stolen password alone is never enough.

Read-only connections

Linked accounts share balance data only. No one can move money, place trades, or change anything in your accounts through the portal.

Routine security testing

eMoney runs ongoing penetration testing and stores client data in geographically separated, secured databases.
