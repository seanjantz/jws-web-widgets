About the author

Sean Jantz

Sean is the founder of Jantz Wealth Solutions and has worked in financial services since 2012. He is an investment advisor representative with a fiduciary duty on the portfolios he advises, and he is insurance licensed, so he can use the right tool for a client's situation rather than the one tool he happens to sell.

He is based in Twin Falls, Idaho, serving families across Southern Idaho and, increasingly, virtually across the country. He wrote this book for the conversations he has every week: clear, direct, and free of the jargon that keeps good people in the dark about their own money.
